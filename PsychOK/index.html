EXPERIENCED, CARING & PASSIONATE

Adelphi Psych Medicine Clinic provides assessment and mental healthcare for students and adults, we specialise in psychotherapy and medico-legal related issues such as mental capacity affecting legal competency.

The clinic offers psychotherapy, an evidence-based therapeutic approach for improving mental health well-being. Psychotherapy is individualized and may be used together with medication. The types of therapy available include:

  • Play Therapy for Children
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • Clinical Psychiatry
  • Clinical Hypnosis
  • Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy

Children learn to understand the world and their place in it through play. It is where they’re free to act out their inner feelings and deepest emotions. Since the child cannot adequately express themselves in the adult world, the therapist joins the child in their world, on their level. As they play, the child may become less guarded and more apt to share their feelings. But they aren’t pressured. They’re allowed to do so in their own time and with their own method of communication. Play therapy will differ depending on the therapist and the particular needs of the child. To begin, the therapist may want to observe the child at play. They may also want to conduct separate interviews with the child, parents, or teachers. After a thorough assessment, the therapist will set some therapeutic goals, decide on what limits may be necessary, and formulate a plan for how to proceed. Much can be revealed in how a child interacts with different types of toys and how their behavior changes from session to session. They may use play to act out fears and anxieties, as a soothing mechanism, or to heal and problem-solve. Each child is different, so therapy will be tailored to their individual needs. As therapy progresses, behaviors and goals can be reassessed. It can help teach conflict resolution, promote healing, and improve family dynamics.
