You are phlegmatic person



A phlegmatic person is a calm and even person, rarely loses his temper, and is not inclined to violent expression of emotions. In activities, he shows thoroughness, thoughtfulness and perseverance. He, as a rule, brings what he started to the end. The behavior of a phlegmatic person is usually characterized by a relatively low level of activity, difficulty in switching and sluggishness.Phlegmatic is not easy to piss off or hurt emotionally. In general, he is patient, calm and friendly person. The phlegmatic person feels best when everything goes as usual. The phlegmatic is the most reliable of all types of temperament. The slowness of mental processes inherent in a phlegmatic person does not allow him to act under the influence of emotions and momentary factors.

A phlegmatic person cannot be called "an easy-going person." Because of his long pauses before answering, it sometimes seems to others that the events taking place around the phlegmatic are not at all interested. Unlike representatives of other temperaments, it is impossible to convince a phlegmatic person with emotions in discussions and negotiations; he perceives only arguments and facts. The phlegmatic's ideas are more productive, he is able to overcome significant difficulties on a long journey to solving a problem.
